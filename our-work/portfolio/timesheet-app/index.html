Timesheet App

Stellenbosch, South Africa

Matogen designed a proprietary timesheet, capacity and pay roll app for Entersekt, a digital security provider.

Key responsibilities included budget management, business analysis, team leadership, and technical consultation. The project encompassed full-cycle development from ERD design and information architecture to UX/UI design for multiple platforms, along with comprehensive technical documentation and quality assurance.

Features

Personal Timesheet Entry

Users can log time against different projects with percentage allocations, view their weekly progress, and add detailed descriptions for their work entries.

Project Management

Track time spent across multiple projects, with the ability to view individual project allocations and overall progress for reporting purposes.

Team Approvals

Review and approve submitted timesheets, with clear progress indicators showing completion status and the ability to manage team members' submissions.

Progress Tracking

Visual progress bars and percentage indicators help users and managers track completion rates and time allocation across different projects and team members.
