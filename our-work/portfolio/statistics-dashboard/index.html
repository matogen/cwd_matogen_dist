Statistics Dashboard

Stellenbosch, South Africa

Matogen designed a statistics dashboard for the Bureau for Economic Research (BER) to visualize South Africa's economic and employment data.

The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into South Africa's economic indicators, including GDP growth, employment statistics, and investment metrics. It features interactive visualizations and detailed analytics to help track and understand economic trends.

Client Requirements

The client needed a user-friendly platform to present complex economic data in an accessible format, enabling users to monitor key economic indicators and trends in South Africa's economy.

Features

Economic Indicators

Real-time display of key economic metrics including GDP growth, public and private fixed investment, and unemployment rates with percentage comparisons.

Interactive Graphs

Time-series visualizations showing historical trends in economic data, including per capita GDP growth rates and economic performance indicators.

Quick Stats Dashboard

At-a-glance view of critical economic metrics with clear visual indicators for positive and negative trends in the economy.

Navigation System

Intuitive sidebar navigation allowing users to explore different economic sectors and topics including employment, corruption, sustainability, and education.
