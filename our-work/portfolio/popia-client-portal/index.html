Popia Client Portal

Midrand, South Africa

BluNova's POPI (Protection of Personal Information) Portal, that includes details of customers from Blue Label Connect, Blue Label Telecoms, Comm Equipment, Striide, Cell-C, Blue Label Media, Transaction Junction, Blue Label Data Solutions, TicketPro, CigiCell, UniPay, BluNova Insights Optimised, Sterring, Talk Financial Services, Reware.

Client Requirements

Needed to provide users with the ability to easily manage their communication preferences, opting in or out of services and communications from various subsidiaries within the Blue Label Telecoms Group.

Features

Check Registration Status

Users can check their registration status and update their details.

Manage Communication Preferences

Users can manage their communication preferences, opting in or out of services and communications from various subsidiaries within the Blue Label Telecoms Group.

Consent and Data Correction Requests

Users can request consent and data correction.

View and Update Personal Information

Users can view and update their personal information.
