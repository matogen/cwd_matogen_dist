Client Requirements

The Trade Order Management System (TOMS) was implemented for Mutual Trust as a web application that is used within the organization and supports Mutual Trust’s day-to-day activities.

Features

Centralized Order Tracking

TOMS enables Mutual Trust employees to efficiently manage and track all client orders from a single platform. It consolidates order information, allowing staff to monitor the status and details of trades placed across different brokers and exchanges.

Multi-Exchange Compatibility

The system supports orders placed on various stock exchanges, providing Mutual Trust with flexibility and access to diverse markets. This feature helps employees manage a wide array of client portfolios and investment options seamlessly.

Broker Integration

TOMS integrates with multiple stock brokers, simplifying the order placement process. This connectivity allows employees to execute trades on behalf of clients without needing to manage multiple broker platforms, streamlining operations and reducing potential errors.

Personalized Client Service Support

By tracking each client’s orders and trades within TOMS, Mutual Trust employees can provide a higher level of personalized service. The system allows easy access to client-specific trade information, helping employees offer customized insights and support based on individual client needs.
