Potchefstroom, South Africa

Matogen designed The Family Constitution platform, a sophisticated family tree and governance system that helps families manage their legacy and relationships.

The platform enables families to document their structure, manage professional advisors, and maintain important family documents and relationships across multiple generations and geographical locations.

Client Requirements

The client needed a comprehensive platform to help families document and manage their legacy across generations. Key requirements included:

The platform needed to visually represent complex family structures while supporting international families with multi-nationality indicators. The interface needed to be intuitive for family members of all ages, with robust export functionality for family tree data and documentation.

Features

Interactive Family Tree

Visual representation of family relationships with interactive member cards showing personal details, nationality indicators, and the ability to expand branches.

Member Management

Comprehensive member profiles with profile pictures, relationship status, nationality flags, and additional information fields for each family member.

Administrative Tools

Dedicated sections for managing documents, contacts, tasks, and professional advisors, with an intuitive navigation system for easy access to all platform features.

Export Functionality

Built-in export capabilities allowing users to save and share family tree data and related information in various formats.
