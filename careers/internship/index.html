The Role

As an Intern at Matogen, you will have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience and contribute to real-world projects under the guidance of experienced professionals. This unpaid role offers a valuable learning opportunity to develop technical skills, industry knowledge, and professional competencies in a supportive and dynamic environment. You will work closely with our team to assist in various tasks and projects, gaining practical insights and building a foundation for a successful career in web development.

Responsibilities

  • Assist in the development and maintenance of web applications, under supervision.
  • Participate in team meetings and discussions to learn about project requirements and objectives.
  • Collaborate with developers, designers, and QA engineers to support project tasks and initiatives.
  • Learn and apply coding standards and best practices in software development.
  • Contribute to testing efforts by executing test cases and reporting bugs.
  • Gain familiarity with version control systems and project management tools used in the industry.
  • Engage in continuous learning and skill development through self-study and mentorship
  • Demonstrate eagerness to learn and take on new challenges in a proactive manner.
  • Communicate effectively with team members and seek guidance when needed.
  • Embrace feedback and use it as an opportunity for growth and improvement.

Experience

  • We prefer university graduates with a 70% or higher average and 5 or more years of experience. We maintain high qualification standards to ensure every team member is a great fit for our organization, clients, and team dynamics.
  • Employment is viewed as a lifelong journey taken together, emphasizing long-term fit and mutual benefit.

While formal education can be valuable, we recognize that talent and skill are not solely confined to those who have pursued traditional academic paths. Therefore, we welcome applicants who possess the requisite skill set and experience, regardless of their educational background.
