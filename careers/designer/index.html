The Role

As a Designer / UX / Front-end Developer at Matogen, you will be at the forefront of creating engaging and intuitive user experiences for our web applications. Your role encompasses both the design and development aspects, where you will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to translate user requirements into visually appealing and functional interfaces. Leveraging your expertise in design principles, user experience methodologies, and front-end development technologies, you will play a key role in shaping the look, feel, and functionality of our web applications to ensure a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with stakeholders to translate user requirements into intuitive design concepts.
  • Develop wireframes and prototypes, iterating based on user feedback.
  • Create visually appealing interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Implement interactive elements and animations to enhance user engagement.
  • Conduct usability testing and incorporate findings into design improvements.
  • Integrate front-end components with back- end logic and databases.
  • Stay updated on design trends and emerging front-end technologies.
  • Ensure alignment with project timelines and quality standards.
  • Provide design guidance and support to development teams.
  • Contribute to the creation and maintenance of design systems.

Experience

  • We prefer university graduates with a 70% or higher average and 5 or more years of experience. We maintain high qualification standards to ensure every team member is a great fit for our organization, clients, and team dynamics.
  • Employment is viewed as a lifelong journey taken together, emphasizing long-term fit and mutual benefit.

While formal education can be valuable, we recognize that talent and skill are not solely confined to those who have pursued traditional academic paths. Therefore, we welcome applicants who possess the requisite skill set and experience, regardless of their educational background.
