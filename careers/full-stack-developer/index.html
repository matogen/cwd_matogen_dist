The Role

As a Full Stack Developer at Matogen, you will play a pivotal role in crafting innovative and dynamic web applications. You'll be responsible for both the front-end and back-end development, ensuring seamless integration of design and functionality. Your expertise will be instrumental in driving the development process from conception to deployment, while collaborating closely with cross- functional teams to deliver high-quality solutions that meet our clients' needs. This role offers an exciting opportunity to leverage your technical skills and creativity to build cutting-edge web experiences that make a tangible impact.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain responsive web applications for optimal performance across devices.
  • Collaborate with designers to translate requirements into functional features.
  • Design and implement server-side logic and APIs for efficient data handling.
  • Write clean, maintainable code following best practices and standards.
  • Troubleshoot and debug issues to ensure smooth application operation.
  • Stay updated on emerging technologies and industry trends.
  • Work with cross-functional teams to deliver projects on time and within scope.
  • Provide technical guidance and support to junior developers.
  • Contribute insights to enhance product features through discussions and code reviews.

Experience

  • We prefer university graduates with a 70% or higher average and 5 or more years of experience. We maintain high qualification standards to ensure every team member is a great fit for our organization, clients, and team dynamics.
  • Employment is viewed as a lifelong journey taken together, emphasizing long-term fit and mutual benefit.

While formal education can be valuable, we recognize that talent and skill are not solely confined to those who have pursued traditional academic paths. Therefore, we welcome applicants who possess the requisite skill set and experience, regardless of their educational background.
