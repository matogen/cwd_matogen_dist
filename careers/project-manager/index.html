The Role

As a Project Manager at Matogen, you will be responsible for overseeing the successful planning, execution, and delivery of web development projects. You will serve as the primary point of contact for clients, ensuring clear communication, managing expectations, and addressing any concerns or issues that arise throughout the project lifecycle. Your leadership will be instrumental in guiding cross-functional teams of developers, designers, and QA engineers to deliver high-quality solutions on time and within budget. This role offers an exciting opportunity to drive project success, foster client relationships, and contribute to the growth and success of our company.

Responsibilities

  • Lead planning, scheduling, and execution of web development projects, aligning with client objectives.
  • Serve as main client contact, maintaining clear communication and managing expectations.
  • Define project scope, objectives, and deliverables, ensuring alignment with stakeholder requirements.
  • Develop and maintain project timelines, budgets, and resource allocations.
  • Coordinate cross-functional teams to ensure project milestones are met.
  • Conduct regular project meetings and updates to keep stakeholders informed.
  • Monitor project progress, identifying opportunities for process improvements.
  • Manage project documentation to ensure accuracy and completeness.
  • Resolve conflicts within the team or with clients, facilitating constructive dialogue.
  • Ensure project deliverables meet quality standards and client expectations through thorough testing and validation.

Experience

  • We prefer university graduates with a 70% or higher average and 5 or more years of experience. We maintain high qualification standards to ensure every team member is a great fit for our organization, clients, and team dynamics.
  • Employment is viewed as a lifelong journey taken together, emphasizing long-term fit and mutual benefit.

While formal education can be valuable, we recognize that talent and skill are not solely confined to those who have pursued traditional academic paths. Therefore, we welcome applicants who possess the requisite skill set and experience, regardless of their educational background.
