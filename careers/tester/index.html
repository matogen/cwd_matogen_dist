The Role

As a Quality Assurance Tester at Matogen, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the functionality, usability, and performance of our web applications meet the highest standards. You will be responsible for meticulously testing every aspect of the software, identifying defects, and providing feedback to developers to ensure a seamless user experience. Your attention to detail and commitment to quality will be instrumental in delivering reliable and bug-free web applications that exceed client expectations.

Responsibilities

  • Develop test plans and cases based on project requirements.
  • Execute manual and automated tests to identify bugs.
  • Conduct functional testing to ensure features meet requirements.
  • Perform regression testing to verify resolved defects.
  • Conduct usability testing to assess user experience.
  • Collaborate with developers to prioritize and resolve issues.
  • Document defects using bug tracking tools.
  • Provide reports on testing progress and defects found.
  • Participate in project meetings to discuss quality assurance.
  • Stay updated on testing methodologies and tools.

Experience

  • We prefer university graduates with a 70% or higher average and 5 or more years of experience. We maintain high qualification standards to ensure every team member is a great fit for our organization, clients, and team dynamics.
  • Employment is viewed as a lifelong journey taken together, emphasizing long-term fit and mutual benefit.

While formal education can be valuable, we recognize that talent and skill are not solely confined to those who have pursued traditional academic paths. Therefore, we welcome applicants who possess the requisite skill set and experience, regardless of their educational background.
